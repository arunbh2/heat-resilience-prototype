4. Intervention logic

High risk: hydration points, shade structures, cool roofs, health-facility preparedness, worker timing protocols.

Medium risk: tree corridors, awareness campaigns, targeted cool-roof pilots, public-space shade.

Low risk: monitoring, public awareness, maintenance of cooling assets, future planting.
