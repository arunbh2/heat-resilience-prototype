Heat Resilience Decision Engine

A simple browser-based tool to test ward-level heat risk, suggest intervention bundles, estimate rough cost bands, and generate a first-draft municipal or donor brief.

This is a prototype. It runs fully in your browser, so it can be hosted on GitHub Pages. Data you add is saved only in your browser unless you export it as CSV.

Decision brief generator

This is the kind of first draft a PA team can copy into a municipal memo, donor note, or internal concept note.

Add a ward, then click Brief in the table or generate a top-3 brief.

Method note: The score uses a transparent weighted formula: heat exposure 30%, population density 15%, informal/low-income housing 15%, shade deficit 15%, health access gap 10%, and outdoor worker exposure 15%. This is not a final scientific model. It is a decision-first prototype that must be corrected with community intelligence and city data.
